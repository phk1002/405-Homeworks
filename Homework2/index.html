Description:

Vegetable Pulao is one of the most popular dishes in India.
Because of the rich flavors and colors in the Vegetable Pulao, both adults and kids enjoy this delicious dish. In my household, Vegetable Pulao is made almost once a week! Usually, vegetable pulao is served with a side of raita (plain yoghurt mixed with onions and herbs).
